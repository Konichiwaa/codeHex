About Dr. Mohammed Moshiul Hoque

Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at CUET, Bangladesh, with a Ph.D. from Saitama University, Japan. Research focuses on NLP, AI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and HCI, particularly for low-resource languages like Bengali.

With over 200 publications and extensive citations, professional roles have included Dean of ECE, Head of CSE, and Director of the Sheikh Kamal IT Business Incubator.
